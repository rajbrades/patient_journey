# Precision Genetic Test (PGT)

## Overview

The Precision Genetic Test analyzes 54 key genetic markers to deliver a personalized wellness roadmap. With more than 700 trillion possible genetic outcomes, proprietary algorithms transform your DNA data into a tailored health, nutrition, and weight management plan as unique as your genome.

This is the world's first Precision Nutrition System — going well beyond standard genetic tests to provide actionable, customized guidance across nutrition, fitness, and lifestyle.

## What It Tests

- **54 genetic markers** across nutrition, weight management, and lifestyle response
- Covers how your body responds to macronutrients, micronutrients, metabolism, and fitness

## Deliverables

Results are delivered as a **~400-page report** divided into three booklets:

| Booklet | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **Precision Plan** | Overall personalized wellness roadmap |
| **Nutritional Analysis** | How your body processes nutrients based on DNA |
| **Weight Analysis** | Genetic insights into weight management and metabolism |

Additional inclusions:
- Access to **10X Health Complete Precision Nutrition Supplements** (custom formulated to your specific genetic makeup)
- Access to **Precision Drip IVs**
- **Precision Food Insights & Recommendations**
- **Precision Weight Management Insights & Recommendations**
