# MitoScreen — Mitochondrial Test

## Overview

The MitoScreen evaluates how efficiently your mitochondria produce and manage energy at the cellular level. Mitochondria are the powerhouses of every cell — their function directly impacts energy, metabolism, brain performance, physical recovery, stress resilience, and healthy aging.

Results deliver actionable insights to optimize lifestyle, nutrition, and supplementation.

## What It Measures

- **Cellular energy production** — how efficiently mitochondria generate ATP
- **Oxidative stress handling** — the body's ability to neutralize free radicals
- **Mitochondrial resilience and adaptability** — response to stressors and recovery demands
- Key markers of **mitochondrial efficiency and performance** via blood analysis

## Who This Test Is For

- Those focused on **athletic performance, longevity, or proactive health optimization**
- Anyone wanting a cellular-level snapshot of how their body is functioning today
