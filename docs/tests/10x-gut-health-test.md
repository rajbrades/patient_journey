# 10X Gut Health Test

## Overview

The 10X Gut Health Test is powered by KBMO Diagnostics' patented **Food Inflammation Test (FIT)** — the only multi-pathway delayed food sensitivity test on the market. It combines the **FIT 176** food sensitivity panel with the proprietary **Gut Barrier Panel (GB Panel)**, providing a comprehensive picture of both food reactivity and gut barrier integrity.

Food sensitivities and related diseases affect at least **100 million people worldwide** and are linked to symptoms ranging from skin rashes and headaches to chronic intestinal diseases.

## What Makes the FIT Test Unique

Unlike standard tests that measure IgG alone, the FIT Test measures both **IgG antibodies and Immune Complexes** — the two most common food-related pathways. Measuring both together increases sensitivity beyond any IgG-only test on the market.

### Gut Barrier Panel (GB Panel)
KBMO's unique panel measures leaky gut across a spectrum using four **gatekeeper markers**:

| Marker | What It Measures | Clinical Significance |
|--------|-----------------|----------------------|
| **Candida** | Candida overgrowth in the stomach/dysbiosis | Precursor to leaky gut occurrence |
| **Zonulin** | Intestinal permeability (leaky gut) | Elevated = compromised tight junction regulation. Unique KBMO marker. |
| **Occludin** | Tight junction stabilization and optimal barrier function | Elevated = tight junctions are breaking down |
| **LPS (Lipopolysaccharides)** | Outer membrane marker of gram-negative bacteria | Elevated = may indicate Leaky Gut Syndrome and GI inflammatory diseases |

The GB Panel is measured using two antibody pathways: **IgG1-4-C3d** and **IgA1-2**.

> The GB Panel is included with both FIT 132 and FIT 176. It is also available as a standalone finger-stick test.
